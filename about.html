About

PhD | Molecular Virology | Nuclear Pore Complex

From 2014 to 2018 I worked on understanding how Kaposi's sarcoma-associated herpesvirus remodelled the nuclear pore complex within human cells under the supervision of Professor Adrian Whitehouse.

Research Software Engineer | University of Leeds

I currently work as a Research Software Engineer in the Research Computing team at the University of Leeds. We help provide training to researchers on reproducible research, using the University's High performance computing system and help foster a collaborative computational research community.

Campaigning | Organising | Winning

Outside of work I volunteer as a Labour party activist in Leeds helping organise to engage with local people in meaningful and constructive ways, using data to drive impactful, targeted and ultimately successful campaigns.
